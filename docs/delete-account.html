Sudoku App - Account Deletion

We value your privacy and make it easy to request the deletion of your account and associated data from our Sudoku app.

To request account deletion, please send an email to: andrewspourgeon@gmail.com with the subject line "Sudoku App - Account Deletion Request"

Information to Include in Your Request

  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your display name in the app (if you remember it)
  • Approximate date when you created the account (if known)

What Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete:

  • Your user profile information
  • Game history and statistics
  • Leaderboard entries
  • Any stored preferences or settings
  • Profile photo (if you provided consent to use it)

Deletion Timeline

We will process your account deletion request within 14 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been removed from our systems.

Data Retention

After deletion, we may retain certain anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you for analytical purposes.

Some information may be retained in our backups for up to 30 days after deletion before being permanently removed during our regular backup rotation.

Additional Information

For more information about how we handle your data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, please contact us at andrewspourgeon@gmail.com.
